Get to Know Me!

My name is Abby Hermanek. I have lived in Nebraska my entire life. I have five people in my family, including myself. I have my mom, dad, younger brother, and twin sister. When I am not spending time with my family you can find me spending time with friends or doing some of my hobbies. I enjoy golfing, cooking, and spending time outdoors.

For high school I attended Marian High School. Marian is an all girls, private, Catholic high school. There I played on the varsity golf team. My senior year our team was State Runner-Up. After graduating I started my college education at UNO. I am currently a junior at the University of Nebraska - Omaha. I plan on getting my degree in Management Information Systems and Accounting.

Some facts that people may not know about me are that I am a twin and that I love anything Disney. Ever since I was born I have been asked the question, "What's it like being a twin?" Everywhere I go my sister and I get this question. My answer is that I am lucky to be one, I wouldn't have it any other way. Another fact some may not know about me is that I love anything Disney. Ever since I was a young kid my family has been going to the Walt Disney World Resort in Orlando, Florida. I've been to Disney ten times, you would think it would get old! Every time I go I try something new, whether it's a thrill ride or restaurant. Disney never gets old, each time we go back we have a different experience.
